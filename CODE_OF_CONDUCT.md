# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks, intimidation, or doxxing.
- Deliberate disruption of community discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block contributors for violations.
